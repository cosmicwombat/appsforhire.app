## How to Log In

All the apps are protected so only authorized people (like you) can access them. When you visit any app for the first time, you'll see a login screen asking for your email address.

1. Enter **tony.fast@gmail.com**
2. Check your inbox for a one-time code (arrives within a minute — check spam if you don't see it)
3. Enter the code and you're in

Your session stays active for **6 hours**, so you won't need to log in again during a normal day of use. After that, just repeat the process — it only takes about 30 seconds.

## Adding to Your Phone (Recommended)

These apps are designed to work great on your phone. To add one to your home screen:

**iPhone (Safari):**
1. Open the app in Safari
2. Tap the Share button (box with arrow at the bottom)
3. Scroll down and tap **Add to Home Screen**
4. Tap **Add**

**Android (Chrome):**
1. Open the app in Chrome
2. Tap the three-dot menu (top right)
3. Tap **Add to Home screen**
4. Tap **Add**

Once added, it opens like a regular app — no browser bar, full screen.
