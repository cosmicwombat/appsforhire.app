FAQ

Questions People Have Actually Asked

What is a PWA, exactly?

A Progressive Web App is a website that behaves like a native app. It works on any device, can be installed on your home screen, loads instantly, and can even work offline. The advantage: no App Store, no downloads, no $99/year Apple developer account. Your users just visit a link — that's it.

What does "byoAI" actually mean in practice?

It means you supply your own AI API key — either from Google (Gemini) or Anthropic (Claude). We build the app that uses it. When a user interacts with the AI features in your app, those API calls are billed directly to your account by Google or Anthropic. We never touch the billing. Both providers offer free tiers that cover light usage — many small apps will never cost you a penny in AI fees.

Do I need to know how to code or manage API keys?

Nope. We'll walk you through getting an API key from Google or Anthropic — it takes about five minutes and is free to start. We handle all the wiring. You just tell us what you want the AI to do.

How does the 2FA protection work?

We use Cloudflare Access to protect your app's URL. Users log in with their email address and receive a one-time code — no password to remember, forget, or get phished. You control who has access to your app. It's the same zero-trust platform used by major enterprises, and it comes with every app we build.

What if I want changes after the app is built?

Minor changes (text, colors, labels) during your included support window are covered at no extra charge. For new features or significant additions, we'll scope and quote the work separately. Hosting subscribers get discounted rates on updates — it's one of the perks of staying on the plan.

What does "buyout" mean?

If you decide you want to take your app somewhere else — host it yourself, hand it to another developer, just have the code — you can buy it out at any time. You'll receive the full source code and we'll help you migrate it wherever you want. No exit fees, no data held hostage.

What happens if I cancel my hosting subscription?

Your app goes offline, but your data and code are safe. You can restart hosting anytime, or buy out the app to self-host. We will never delete your work without 30 days' notice and explicit confirmation from you.

Do I need my own domain name?

Nope — your app lives on a secure subdomain we provide (like yourname.appsforhire.app). If you have your own domain and want to use it, we can set that up as part of the build at no extra charge.

How do I pay?

Build fees are collected upfront via Stripe before development begins. Monthly hosting is billed automatically via Stripe subscription. We accept all major credit and debit cards. No weird payment portals. No wire transfers. No invoices "due in 30 days" that you'll lose in your inbox.

What are the limits here? What do you not build?

We're a boutique shop — we build focused, single-purpose apps that solve specific problems. We're not the right fit for full SaaS products, apps requiring dedicated server infrastructure, or anything needing real-time multi-user collaboration at scale. If your idea sounds like it needs a 10-person engineering team, we'll tell you honestly and point you in the right direction.
